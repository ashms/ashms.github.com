Leadership Roles

Laura Zeiler

Team Captain

Laura is a senior studying Industrial and Operations Engineering. Prior to joining her sophomore year, she never anticipated being so excited about concrete. Outside of MCCT, Laura is also involved in Central Student Government and IM Sports. As captain, she is eager to see what innovation and progress this hard working team can accomplish this year.

Eric O'Neill

Concrete Design

Eric is from Farmington Hills, Michigan and is a Junior studying Chemical Engineering with a Minor in Computer Science. In his role as the 2016-2017 Mix Design Lead he plans to strengthen the concrete mix while also strengthening the team. This is his third year on the team and last year he held the position of Paddling Lead. Outside of Concrete Canoe, Eric enjoys playing IM sports, hanging out with his friends, and watching Michigan Football.
